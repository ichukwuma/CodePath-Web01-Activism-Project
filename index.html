Hunger and food insecurity in the U.S

Hunger and food insecurity is a very prevalent issue in the U.S., which is especially common among lower income families.

Source: Context youtube channel.

About My Topic

What many people fail to realize is that food is one of the most pressing issues in the U.S. among lower income families, especially since they may be living pay-check to pay-check, have low savings, and may be set back in cash due to unexpected financial crisis/emergencies. According to Feeding America more than 34 million people in the U.S.,suffer from food insecurity. Due to the recent pandemic (COVID-19), food insecurity has increased within communites of color and rural communities. Even though these communities are suffering from such problems, many still do not qualify for federal nutrition programs that are created to help families struggling with food insecurity.
